Angelo Cappellini - Фабрика

Story

In Lombardy, halfway between Milan – the capital of design, fashion, and finance – and Lake Como with all its beauty, you’ll find the Brianza region, which has always been the historical centre of Italian art furniture. At the centre of Brianza, there’s Cabiate. This is a little town where way back in 1886, Angelo Cappellini was founded, a company known around the world for its recreation of the most elegant furnishing styles of the past, as well as the modern tone of the Opera collections, defined by a contemporary and sophisticated design. Even centuries ago, the town of Cabiate was a point of reference for the production of high artisan furnishings, offering a number of “workshops”, expert inlayers, and above all master wood carvers and engravers. Among the pioneers in this area, Angelo Cappellini arose at the end of the 19th century, focusing on the combination of artisan care and industry organization – a formula that is still valid to this day.

PRODUCTION

Over 12,000 square meters of production space represent the identity of Angelo Cappellini, a company that wonderfully maintains that familiar vitality and calm feel – this is the place where experience and knowledge are stored, and where high added value and hard work are carried out. This area is enhanced by the additional of 2,000 square meters of elegant showroom, where you can see and touch the quality of these creations. Today the company offers three huge complete style proposals suitable for residential and contract; Angelo Cappellini, collections for hospitality and the abode with classic taste, which are defined by the knowledge of traditional techniques and high quality workmanship. Opera Contemporary, here you will find offers for a way of contemporary living that is cool and fashionable, where the use of unparalleled materials and precious wood types are combined and mixed with styles and tradition with glamorous ingredients, appealing to an increasingly wide audience.Mediterraneo, a new interpretation of the classic style that is fresh and light, inspired by Provencal style – a production that embraces furniture in all its entirety, renewed in a lifestyle that fully reflects the unparalleled elegance of the Made in Italy.

Service

Angelo Cappellini produces from the single standard collection on the catalogue to turnkey projects with the guarantee of furniture that is truly works of art, following the modus operandi that has always distinguished this company. From an initial briefing where we discuss the project with the customer, to creation and concept of the project, following the drafting of the detailed estimate, depending on the requirements and the entity that the project may include, as well as inspections, implementation and final checks. Once approved, we move to the final executive project, where the client will view the technical designs, materials and finishes. The final inspection in the company prior to delivery to the customer guarantees the quality of the product, designed and created made specifically for the project at hand. This is how a project comes to completion, where everything is carried out with a single expert partner: Angelo Cappellini. Angelo Cappellini’s professionalism stands for the perfect partner, because it guarantees, for each and every project, privacy and the utmost care in the management of the project, ensuring the customer a product that is truly customized, exclusive and one-of-a-kind.
