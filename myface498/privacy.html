4. Cookies.

Please note that we collect certain information from your browser and devices using cookies. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or other internet-connected device in order to identify your browser, provide analytics, and remember some information about you such as your login information when you access the Application and use the Services.

Information we obtain using cookies includes, among other, IP address, geolocation data, user/client ID, activities.

We use this information to make the functionality of our Application available to you, to keep you logged in (if accepted by you), to carry out analytics and statistics, for marketing purposes.

We may use both session cookies and persistent cookies. Session cookies expire when you close your browser automatically. A persistent cookies will remain until expire or you delete your cookies.

We may use third-party cookies set by the companies who provide analytics and statistics services to us. Please note that this Privacy Policy covers only our use of cookies and does not include use of cookies by third parties.

You can change your browser options as to handling cookies. If you do not accept or reject cookies, you may not be able to access some features of our Application and we recommend that you leave them turned on.
